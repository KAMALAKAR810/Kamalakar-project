How to Play

  1. Login to your account: Ensure you are logged in to access the betting markets.
  2. Add Balance: Go to the Payment page to add points to your wallet.
  3. Select a Game: Choose from Single, Jodi, or Patti games from the navigation menu.
  4. Choose Market & Session: Select your preferred market (e.g., Kalyan) and session (Open or Close).
  5. Enter Numbers & Amounts: Enter the amount you want to bet against your chosen numbers.
  6. Place Prediction: Click on "Place Predictions" to confirm your bet.
  7. Check History: Monitor your bets and winnings in the History section.
